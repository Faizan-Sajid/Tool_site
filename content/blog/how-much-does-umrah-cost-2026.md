## Umrah Cost from Pakistan 2026 — Full Breakdown

Pakistan has one of the largest Umrah-performing populations in the world. Prices vary by departure city, season, and whether you travel with a government-approved or independent operator.

### Economy Umrah from Pakistan

Economy Umrah from Pakistan in 2026 costs between PKR 220,000 and PKR 300,000 for a 7–10 night trip outside Ramadan. These packages typically include return flights from Karachi, Lahore, or Islamabad, quad-occupancy hotel accommodation within 1–2 km of Masjid al-Haram, and the Umrah visa fee.

Standard packages with better hotels and smaller rooms run PKR 350,000–500,000. Haram-view rooms and five-star hotels push costs to PKR 700,000–900,000+ per person.

### Ramadan Umrah from Pakistan

During Ramadan, Umrah packages from Pakistan increase sharply. The first 20 nights of Ramadan cost PKR 400,000–600,000. The last ten nights — the Laylatul Qadr period — regularly exceed PKR 700,000–900,000 per person. Book at least 6–8 months in advance for any Ramadan package.

**2026 visa rule change:** The Umrah visa entry window is now 30 days from issuance, reduced from 90 days. Extensions are not permitted. Plan your travel dates carefully.

**Umrah cost from Pakistan in 2026 starts at PKR 220,000 for an economy package outside Ramadan.** Ramadan packages cost 30–50% more. The visa fee is SAR 300, approximately PKR 22,300. Pakistani passport holders must apply through a licensed operator — direct Nusuk applications are not available for Pakistani nationals.

## Umrah Cost from India 2026

Umrah from India costs roughly INR 85,000–1,50,000 for a standard economy 7–10 night package outside Ramadan. Premium packages with Haram-view hotels and better flights cost INR 2,00,000–3,50,000.

India has a large number of licensed Umrah operators. Prices vary significantly between operators — compare at least three quotes before booking. Flying from Mumbai or Delhi typically gives cheaper options than from smaller cities due to more direct Jeddah and Madinah flight connections.

**Umrah cost from India in 2026 starts at INR 85,000 for an economy package.** Ramadan packages exceed INR 4,00,000 for the last ten nights. Compare quotes from at least three Hajj Committee of India approved operators before booking.

## Umrah Cost from UK 2026

Umrah from the UK costs £1,500–£3,000 for a standard economy package during off-peak months. Ramadan packages, particularly for the last ten nights, regularly exceed £4,000–£6,000 per person.

British Pakistani pilgrims often find better package value through operators based in Birmingham, Bradford, or Manchester who have direct hotel relationships in Makkah and Madinah. For UK pilgrims, direct Nusuk registration is available at [nusuk.sa](https://www.nusuk.sa) — you do not need to go through a national authority.

**Umrah cost from the UK in 2026 starts at £1,500 for an economy off-peak package.** Standard packages run £3,000–£5,000. Ramadan last-ten-nights packages exceed £6,000. UK pilgrims can register directly on the Nusuk platform without a ministry intermediary.

## Umrah Cost from USA and Canada 2026

Economy Umrah from the USA runs $2,000–$4,000 including flights. During Ramadan, expect $4,000–$7,000+ for a decent package. Canada is broadly similar — often slightly higher due to fewer direct flight options from cities outside Toronto and Vancouver.

US-based pilgrims can apply directly through the Nusuk platform. There is no government Hajj authority in the USA, so all bookings go through Nusuk-approved private operators.

**Umrah cost from the USA in 2026 starts at $2,000 for an economy package.** Ramadan packages run $4,000–$8,000+. For Canada, budget CAD 2,800–5,000 for economy travel outside Ramadan.

## When Is Umrah Cheapest in 2026? — Season-by-Season Guide

Timing is the single most powerful way to reduce Umrah cost. The same hotel, the same package tier, the same operator — prices can differ by 50% depending purely on when you travel.

| Season | Months | Relative Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rajab / Shaban (off-peak) | Jan – Mar 2026 | ⬇️ Lowest | Best value, cooler weather, smaller crowds |
| Muharram / Safar | Oct – Nov 2026 | ⬇️ Low–Medium | Good availability post-Hajj |
| Dhul Qa'dah / Dhul Hijjah | Sep – Oct 2026 | ➡️ Medium | Avoid Hajj dates (8–13 Dhul Hijjah) |
| Ramadan first 20 nights | Feb – Mar 2026 | ⬆️ High | 30–50% premium over off-peak |
| Last 10 nights Ramadan | Mar 2026 | ⬆️⬆️ Very High | 2–3x standard prices |

**The cheapest time for Umrah in 2026 is Rajab and Shaban — roughly January to March outside Ramadan.** Prices are 30–40% lower than Ramadan rates. Avoid the last ten nights of Ramadan if budget is the priority — they are the most expensive period of the entire year.

## How to Get Umrah Visa Online 2026 — Step-by-Step

As of 2026, the Umrah visa process has been simplified for most nationalities through the official Nusuk platform.

1. **Create a Nusuk account** at [nusuk.sa](https://www.nusuk.sa) — requires email, passport copy, and photo.
2. **Select your travel dates** — Umrah can be performed any time except Hajj season (Dhul Hijjah 8–13).
3. **Choose an approved package** — packages include accommodation, transport, and visa processing.
4. **Pay online** — visa fee is SAR 300, approximately $80 or PKR 22,300. Payment via credit or debit card.
5. **Receive e-visa** — typically within 24–72 hours. Print or save PDF for immigration.

**The Umrah visa fee in 2026 is SAR 300 — approximately $80, £63, PKR 22,300, or INR 6,700.** The entry window is now 30 days from issuance. Extensions are not permitted. Pakistani, Indian, and Bangladeshi passport holders must apply through a licensed operator, not directly through Nusuk.

> **Important 2026 Update:** Women aged 45 and above can perform Umrah without a mahram when travelling in a group through a licensed operator. Women under 45 still require a mahram. Meningococcal vaccination (within 3 years) is mandatory for all pilgrims.
